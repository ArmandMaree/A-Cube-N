unclutter It's you. Simplified. Want to know more? Click the video below. Or go ahead and click here to try Unclutter out for yourself,
User Experience We have placed a big emphisis on our user interface to give you the best possible experience while enjoying Unclutter. Interactive bubbles and beautifully designed menus gives you a easy and intuitive way to interact with our application. We even give you the option to change what colours you do not like. It's you, personalised.

Our API

When you sign in, you get the option to select three datasources that will be displayed in the interactive Bubble Map. Are you not happy with the options we give you or would you like to see your favourite social media in Unclutter? We give you the option to write your own plugin! You, as a developer, need only to interact with our seamless API and write your own application for your favourite social media. This will then plug into our system like a puzzle piece. It is really that simple. It's you, customised.
Real Time Computation Once you have signed into a your social media, everything that happens on our side will happen in real time. Just recieved an email or did someone just comment on your post? Reload your graph and see how the new information relates to your already loaded bubbles. It's you, optimised.
Your information right where you want it We have designed our application towards how your brain thinks, in concepts. Just how your mind would extrapolate information from your social media, we too have built our application for creating links between topics and how every topic relates with one another. This doesnt help you much unless we give you the data that goes along with that topic, which we have. Double click on any topic you would like to see the emails or posts for and the side bar will expand to give you all the information we collected for that topic. Yuo can even do nifty things like commenting on a post from facebook or retweeting a tweet from twitter, all from Unclutter. Its you, combined.
